# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: February 28, 2026**

Cadence ("the app") is developed by Sampath Mora. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

## Data Collection

Cadence does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All information used by the app stays entirely on your device.

## Health Data

Cadence requests read-only access to the following data types from Apple Health:

- **Step count** — to display your daily and weekly step totals
- **Heart rate** — to calculate time spent in your custom heart rate zones

This data is:
- Read directly from Apple Health on your device
- Never uploaded to any server
- Never shared with third parties
- Never used for advertising or analytics

Cadence does not write any data back to Apple Health.

## Data Storage

The only data Cadence stores locally on your device is:

- Your custom heart rate zone configurations (name, BPM range, weekly target)
- Your daily step goal

This is saved using iOS standard storage (UserDefaults) and never leaves your device.

## Third-Party Services

Cadence uses no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or crash reporting services.

## Children's Privacy

Cadence does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
